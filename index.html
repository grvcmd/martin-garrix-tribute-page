Martin Garrix

Here is a time line of Martin Garrix's life:

  • 1996: Martijn Gerard Garritsen is born in Amstelveen.
  • 2004: Expressed interest in Djing after seeing DJ Tiesto perform at that year's Summer Olympics Opening Ceremony in Athens
  • 2013: Graduates from Herman Brood Academy
  • 2013: Releases his first song "BFAM" with Julian Jordan and becomes gains a massive following after his hit single "Animals"
  • 2016: Launches his own record label called Stmpd Rcrds
  • 2016: Named the world's top DJ
  • 2019:Releases more top songs such as "No Sleep" and "These Are The Times
  • Present: Martin continues to headline festivals, breaking records and giving people the time of their lives.

Garrix’s love of electronic music and festival culture hasn’t diminished over the years; when he is playing, he’s doing it for fun, not fame.
